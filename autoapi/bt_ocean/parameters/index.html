bt_ocean.parameters

A set of base model parameters. Physical parameters (excluding the viscous parameter, bottom drag, and density) are from

  • David P. Marshall and Alistair J. Adcroft, ‘Parameterization of ocean eddies: Potential vorticity mixing, energetics and Arnold’s first stability theorem’, Ocean Modelling 32(3-4), pp. 188–204, 2010, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ocemod.2010.02.001
